QN, 2011-2015

ðŸ”µ Java, OpenGL, LWJGL

The codebase of this spanned almost 10 years.
Huge project. Aim was to makea 2D indie sidescroller, action like Metroid but with RPG elements.
Story was written, locations were defined and designed, most characters were finished.
Code that I wrote in Essence 2007 and Black Clouds 2008 eventually became Quantum Nucleus.
Now of course this was multiple complete rewrites moving from Java2D to OpenGL and then to newer bindings with shaders and and such. Uses the same bindings as Minecraft LWJGL for Java OpenGL.

Ultimately this project died due to scope. Especially making spirtes and animations, that looked good, was a herculean task.

Alkahest, 2013

ðŸ”µ Java, OpenGL, LWJGL

While working on Quantum Nucleus, a better and better map editor was needed.

Flocking Boids, 2009

ðŸ”µ Java2D

Simulation application which simulates the flocking behavior of birds.
Uses the Boids algorithm to simulate the flocking behavior of birds.
Birds are attracted to each other and repelled by each other, and they try to match the velocity of their neighbors.
It uses the 3 rules of separation, alignment, and cohesion.
